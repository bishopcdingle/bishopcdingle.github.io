About the Bishop

A Life Defined by the Word of God

Bishop Dr. Charles W. Dingle has devoted over 53 years to pastoral ministry, biblical scholarship, and theological education. His journey has been forged not only in the halls of scholarship but in the crucible of ministry and intimate moments of spiritual wrestling with the Word of God.

"The soul lies at the absolute center of our identity, intricately woven into our relationship with the Creator and our fellow man."

Through fervent prayer, exhaustive study, and deep personal experience, Bishop Dingle has reached a non-negotiable conviction that the Church urgently needs to hear.

True Standard

Christian Bible College & Seminary

The True Standard Christian Bible College and Seminary was founded by Bishop Dr. Charles W. Dingle as an institution committed to one standard — the uncompromising truth of the Word of God.

Located in Tarboro, North Carolina, the seminary offers undergraduate and graduate programs in biblical studies, theology, homiletics, and philosophy — equipping the next generation of spiritual leaders.
